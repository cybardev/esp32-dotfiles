# ESP32-C3 Configs

Scripts for _my_ ESP32-C3 board with 0.42" OLED display.

> [!NOTE]
> Given the variety of hardware even among the ESP32-C3 boards, the scripts here may or may not work for your board. Please proceed with caution.
